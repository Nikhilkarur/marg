🎙️ Scream Fingerprint Tool

Captures the exact spectral signature of your demo scream clip — the speaker, the room, and this mic — so the Guardian fires reliably on stage.

  1. Open this on the same device + same origin you'll demo with (e.g. localhost:5173/fingerprint.html).
  2. Press Start capture, allow the mic, then play your scream clip out loud near the laptop.
  3. It grabs the loudest ~moment and saves a fingerprint. Open the app — the Guardian now matches it.
